Aston Martin DBS 770 Ultimate Specifications & Features

The 2023 Aston Martin DBS 770 Ultimate is a tribute to the brand’s century-old legacy that reflected the nostalgic past to the modern era. The luxury British supercar maker presented a limited edition Aston Martin DBS 770, the company’s most powerful production car yet. The new DBS 770 Ultimate is a beast in all senses and a beauty when it comes to its mystique design. The Aston offers a magnificent 758-hp V12 engine and offers a crazy acceleration from 0-60 in a crazy 3.2 seconds. The new DBS will be a limited-run model with just 499 examples of both Coupe and Convertible body styles starting from $387,600. The limited edition DBS 770 Ultimate is already sold out so good luck finding one for yourself.

Specifications, 0-60 MPH, Top-speed & Performance

The new Aston Martin DBS 770 Ultimate packs a powerful 5.2L Naturally Aspirated V12 engine that makes an astounding 759 hp and 684 lb-ft of torque. The DBS 770 can accelerate from 0-60 mph in 3.2 seconds and offers a top speed of over 211 mph. The limited-edition Aston comes with a sporty ZF 8-speed auto gearbox with rear-wheel drive and a mechanical limited-slip differential system. The car uses standard carbon-ceramic brakes and offers a whole of upgrades in terms of handling. The Aston provides a thrilling ride and offers incredible athleticism with a lot of fun.

Mileage

Aston has not shared any details about their latest DBS 770 Ultimate’s mileage and fuel efficiency. According to the mileage figures of the DBS Superleggera, the new DBS 770 offers a fuel economy of 12 mpg in the city and 20 mpg on the highway.
