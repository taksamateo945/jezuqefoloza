Who Is John Stamos?

Full name, John Phillip Stamos, was born on August 19, 1963, in Cypress, California, the United States to William ‘Bill’ Stamos and Loretta. His father was of Greek descent, and his mother was of Irish, English, and German ancestry. Additionally, John has two sisters, Alaina and Janeen.

Stamos was educated at John F. Kennedy High School and later enrolled at Cypress College in 1981 but dropped out of his studies to pursue his career as an actor.

John was married to an ex-wife. Rebecca Romijn from 1998 to 2005. After being single for years, the Full House actor started dating Caitlin McHugh in 2016 and eventually got married in 2018. He now is the father of an infant son with his second wife born in 2018.

How Did John Stamos Make His Name in the Entertainment Industry?

John Stamos is one of the renowned actors in Hollywood. Stamos made his acting debut in the television series ‘Dreams’ in which he played the role of Gino Minnelli. Later, he appeared in a television movie ‘Alice in Wonderland.’ Then Stamos landed a recurring role as Matt Willows in the television series ‘You Again?’

He got a big beak in the year 1984 and played the character Blackie Parrish in the daytime drama ‘General Hospital’ until 1992. For his role, he also nominated for a Daytime Emmy Award.

After working a few movies and televisions, John again was able to make an impact on people’s lives as Jessie on ABC’s hit series Full House released in 1995. Moreover, he is also known for playing Dr. Tony Fated in the medical drama ‘ER.’

He also had tried his hand on the big screen as Lance Stargrove in the 1986 movie ‘Never Too Young to Die’ along with the late 80s singer Denise Katrina Matthews better known as Vanity.

Additionally, he also had appearances in shows such as Born to Ride, Knots, They Came Together, Friends, Tow and a Half Men, and many more.
