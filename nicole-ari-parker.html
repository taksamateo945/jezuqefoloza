Nicole Ari Parker Biography

Nicole Ari Parker is best known as Actress, Fashion Model, Producer who has an estimated Net Worth of $5 Million. Actress who played Becky Barnett in the dramatic film Boogie Nights. In 2006, she won an Emmy Award for her MADtv song “A Wonderfully Normal Day.” She was born on October 7,1970, Baltimore, MD. She had her breakthrough role on the TV show Soul Food, where she met her future husband. She was born an only child and won the Best Actress Award at Maryland's high school competition when she was 17 years old. She appeared in Boogie Nights, which also featured Mark Wahlberg. She married actor Boris Kodjoe in 2005; they had children, Nicolas and Sophie together. Her zodiac sign is Libra.
