Real Housewives Of Atlanta: Porsha Williams and Marlo Hampton finally patch up faltering friendship

 Posted on June 4, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1624 words  |  Martina Birk

Porsha Williams and Marlo Hampton finally repaired their friendship on Sunday’s episode of The Real Housewives of Atlanta.

During a cast trip to New Orleans organized by Drew Sidora, 35, the two old friends had an honest heart-to-heart in the unlikeliest of places.

‘Today, we’re in this space, it’s a f***ing healing twerk center, and we gonna figure this s*** out,’ promised Porsha, 39, as the two of them hugged.

Friends again: Porsha Williams and Marlo Hampton finally repaired their friendship on Sunday’s episode of The Real Housewives of Atlanta

The women had just taken a class that combined Zydeco movement with twerking, or ‘shaking,’ as they called it in the Big Easy.

But it was Marlo’s recent movements Porsha was concerned with—especially since things had changed between them since the last cast trip, to Charleston.

On that occasion, they had all celebrated Cynthia Bailey’s upcoming nuptials with a raucous party at which at least half the group got drunk.

Over the course of the night, Porsha was rumored to have had a threesome with pal Tanya Sam, 42, and a stripper.

Candid conversation: During a cast trip to New Orleans, Marlo and Porsha had an honest heart-to-heart in the unlikeliest of places

No one was more devoted to spreading this rumor than Kenya Moore, 50, who interrogated cast members about it and wouldn’t let it go.

When Marlo, 45, and Kenya revived their friendship during the trip, Porsha was understandably hurt—and it all came to a head in New Orleans.

When the class was over, Porsha asked Marlo to stay behind in the ‘nice healing space.’

Zydeco class: The women had just taken a class that combined Zydeco movement with twerking, or ‘shaking,’ as they called it in the Big Easy

She asked her why she never called her anymore, and Marlo suggested it wasn’t intentional.

‘I am a friend to you, and I feel you don’t trust me being a friend to someone you’re not friends with,’ Marlo said.

She pointed out that Porsha’s previous support of Kenya was what made her consider making up with the haircare mogul.

Trust issues: ‘I am a friend to you, and I feel you don’t trust me being a friend to someone you’re not friends with,’ Marlo said

‘But the night you made friends with her was the night she made that accusation,’ Porsha noted.

‘I’m going to ask you: Everything she said, was it false?’ Marlo wondered.

‘Yes!’ Porsha said. ‘Do you think that any of my friends had to ask me what you just asked me?’

Real friend: ‘Yes!’ Porsha said. ‘Do you think that any of my friends had to ask me what you just asked me?’

‘I have no idea,’ Marlo confessed.

‘She’s doing it to you because she knows she can manipulate you,’ Porsha emphasized. ‘This is not an epiphany. She’s been doing this s*** for three weeks straight.’

Marlo promised to stand up for Porsha if the topic of the threesome arose again—and she apologized for having been distant that month.

Promise made: Marlo promised to stand up for Porsha if the topic of the threesome arose again—and she apologized for having been distant that month

‘In my head, I’m thinking the same thing you’re thinking: ‘She f***ing just threw me away because I went and made up with another girl,’ Marlo relayed, expressing how much their separation upset her, too. ‘I want you to know I love you….’

Porsha insisted that they would figure it out and start fresh.

‘And b****, don’t be actin’ like you mad at me about Kenya no more,’ Marlo said.

Making up: Porsha was worried about her friendship with Marlo during the group trip

She and Porsha got on a bus with the cast to go to their next activity.

Kenya immediately noticed that her new bestie Marlo seemed upset.

‘It was a lot of energy,’ Marlo told Kenya. ‘Give me two more cocktails and I can talk to y’all about it.’

Trip organizer: Drew Sidora organized the group trip to New Orleans

The discussion was just one of the areas of tension among castmates that showed up on the NOLA trip.

The ladies had initially flown there on a private plane Drew’s husband Ralph rented for them.

This alone was a point of contention, with Kenya Tweeting after the show: ‘I pay for my own [private jets] and she lies and says it was her husband when it was production.’

Private jet: This alone was a point of contention, with Kenya Tweeting after the show: ‘I pay for my own [private jets] and she lies and says it was her husband when it was production’

Driving into the city, the cast teased Kenya about her friendship reunion with Marlo, which set off Kenya’s close friend LaToya Ali, 34.

‘I’m gonna get Kenya back and have Porsha on the side,’ LaToya joked, making sly reference to her alleged sapphic hook-ups with both women.

‘You better know who the side chick is,’ Kenya snapped back.

Side chick: ‘You better know who the side chick is,’ Kenya snapped back

‘You ain’t never have me on the side,’ Porsha protested. ‘If they stray, they can go away. Okay?’

When the women arrived at their hotel, they found that Drew had arranged for them to be greeted by a brass band and served po’boys and cocktails.

Drew gave Cynthia Bailey, 54, the best room, to make up for not singing at her wedding.

Brass band: When the women arrived at their hotel, they found that Drew had arranged for them to be greeted by a brass band and served po’boys and cocktails

The second-best room was assigned to the woman who did the best ‘booty bounce,’ which turned out to be Porsha.

Kenya refused to compete and headed to the lobby, where she booked her own suite.

Despite Drew telling everyone they could bring their babies along, only Kenya had brought her daughter Brooklyn, two.

No contest: Kenya refused to compete in the ‘booty bounce’ and headed to the lobby, where she booked her own suite

She may have regretted that later as Brooklyn colored everything in the room with crayons.

Following check-in, the group took a bike tour along the Mississippi River and into the French Quarter, where they headed straight for Acme Oyster House.

They dined on fried oysters, alligator bites and ‘boo fries’ — like the Southern version of poutine, swathed in gravy and cheese.

Bike tour: Following check-in, the group took a bike tour along the Mississippi River and into the French Quarter, where they headed straight for Acme Oyster House

Kenya was for once having a blast and letting go, which the ladies enjoyed.

But LaToya was feeling ‘frustrated’ for some reason and that led her to act out.

She had often irritated the cast with her drunken antics, and was suddenly not drinking.

Feeling frustrated: LaToya was feeling ‘frustrated’ for some reason and that led her to act out

When Drew repeatedly asked her why she wasn’t, LaToya drummed on the table and chanted, ‘I just don’t feel like it, b****.’

‘I’m so unbothered, because she’s fake,’ Drew told the table. ‘She’s fake as he**.’

The Game actress explained that after Shamea Morton’s most recent party, her driver Danny gave LaToya a ride home and had her in tears as she realized she needed to cut her drinking.

Drinking questions: When Drew repeatedly asked her why she wasn’t drinking, LaToya drummed on the table and chanted, ‘I just don’t feel like it, b****’

‘You said you wanted to fast, you wanted to detox, so I was on board helping you and supporting you,’ Drew recalled. ‘And then the next time I saw you, you were drinking.’

Kenya wondered why Drew was putting LaToya ‘on blast’ if she was helping her.

‘When I’m around you ladies, y’all can drink and drink and drink,’ LaToya admitted. ‘I try to keep up with y’all, but I can’t.’

On blast: Kenya wondered why Drew was putting LaToya ‘on blast’ if she was helping her

She insisted that she only got wild around the cast, and that she did not have a problem.

LaToya and Kenya then headed back to the hotel in a car as the rest of the cast partied on.

Late that night, Kenya tried to convince Cynthia that Drew had ‘bullied’ LaToya, and both women agreed that LaToya was not an alcoholic.

No problem: LaToya insisted that she only got wild around the cast, and that she did not have a problem

In Drew’s room, Porsha and Shamea, 38, discussed the fact that Kenya and Marlo seemed to have become besties overnight, which distressed Porsha.

She’d thought she and Marlo were becoming close, but now her pal had disappeared on her.

The next day, Drew enlisted her ‘glam squad’ to help her get ready for a radio interview and a meeting about joining a TV series.

Glam squad: The next day, Drew enlisted her ‘glam squad’ to help her get ready for a radio interview and a meeting about joining a TV series

Radio interview: Drew also had a radio interview in New Orleans

Kandi Burruss, 44, arrived after taking a break from the show she was shooting and joined everyone at the hotel for breakfast.

LaToya sat outside with dark sunglasses, claiming she had her ‘shade blockers’ on and observing that Drew had set out alcohol, which she thought hypocritical.

When it started to rain, Drew said, ‘They say it’s a hurricane coming, but we gonna rebuke that.’

In a preview of next week’s episode, Hurricane Zeta was shown slamming New Orleans—the strongest hurricane to ever hit the city.

Hurricane coming: When it started to rain, Drew said, ‘They say it’s a hurricane coming, but we gonna rebuke that’
