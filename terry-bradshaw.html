Terry Bradshaw Age, Biography

Terry Bradshaw is currently 74 years old having been born on September 2, 1948, in Shreveport, Louisiana, USA.  He was raised alongside two brothers by his mother and father, a US Navy veteran, who set the ambition of becoming a professional football player early in childhood.

Terry attended Louisiana Tech University after demonstrating his talent in high school. He graduated from Louisiana Tech University and was picked first overall by the Pittsburgh Steelers.

Although he spent the first two seasons as the backup quarterback to Phil “Roxie” Robertson, Bradshaw got his opportunity when the former retired from football to focus on the Duck Commander organization.

Robertson eventually became a reality TV personality, appearing on “Duck Dynasty.”

Terry Bradshaw Wife

Bradshaw’s first marriage was to Melissa Babish, who was Miss Teen America in 1969. They married in 1972 and divorced a year later. He then married JoJo Starbuck in 1976 before divorcing her in 1983. Charla Hopkins, his family’s attorney, was his third wife.

The pair married in 1983 and had two children before divorcing in 1999. Bradshaw married his fourth and current wife, Tammy, in 2014.

Terry’s Mental Health Problems

Terry Bradshaw revealed after retiring from football that he had suffered from severe mental health problems throughout his career. These concerns included frequent sobbing bouts, weight loss, insomnia, and terrible anxiety attacks.

These problems were exacerbated following his third divorce. He feels that by coming out about depression, he can help alleviate some of the stigma associated with the condition.

Despite this, his anxiety attacks prevented him from ever returning to a Steelers game following his retirement. These panic attacks also prompted him to skip the funeral of team founder Art Rooney.
