Aston Merrygold Reveals 'Precious' EP Tracklist

 Posted on June 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  254 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Two days ago on April 12th, former JLS hunk Aston Merrygold revealed that his highly anticipated debut EP titled ‘Precious’ would be released this month. The exciting announcement came after the 29-year-old singer released the sun-kissed music video for his brand new single also called ‘Precious’.

Now the handsome singer, dancer and actor has officially revealed the tracklist for the ‘Precious’ EP which promises to be filled with some seriously fresh tunes.

The record contains four brand new songs along with the single ‘Precious’ which features rapper Shy Carter and also and acoustic version of his latest single which is definitely his best release to date.

The full ‘Precious’ EP tracklist is as follows:

Precious (feat. Shy Carter)
Distraction
The Favourite
When a Woman Breaks a Man’s Heart
You Me And The Moon
Precious (feat. Shy Carter) [Acoustic]
