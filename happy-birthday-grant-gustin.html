The fastest hero and the charming dancer/singer just turned 26 today. We’re talking about the heart-throbbing sensation and an all-rounder celebrity, Grant Gustin.

Thomas Grant Gustin, better known as Grant Gustin, is an American actor and singer/dancer. He was born on January 14, 1990, in Norfolk, Virginia.

He is known for his roles as Barry Allen/Flash on the CW series The Flash and as Sebastian Smythe on the Fox series Glee.

He also appeared on the CW series Arrow, for cross-over episodes. Apart from that, he appeared on 90120 and CSI: Miami.
