Who Are the Members of Social Media Star Cameron Dallas’ Family?

Cameron Dallas is a social media star who can boast of 5.4 million subscribers on YouTube, 21.5 million followers on Instagram and 8.1 million fans on TikTok. He is the winner of four Teen Choice Awards. 

Date of Birth: September 8, 1994

Zodiac Sign: Virgo 

Cameron got interested in social media in 2012, when he started posting videos on Vine app. He proceeded with gaining popularity on other platforms, including YouTube and Instagram, and in 2016, he began starring in the Netflix series Chasing Cameron. Dallas also tried his hand at acting in Expelled and The Outfield films along with making a Broadway debut with Mean Girls in 2020. Additionally, he deals with modeling and singing.   

Now, it’s time to have a look at the family of Cameron Dallas. 

Cameron Dallas parents

Dan Dallas (father)

Date of Birth: April 16

Zodiac Sign: Aries

Dan was estranged from his son for many years and didn’t take an active part in his upbringing. On 2015 Christmas, Cameron spent the whole day with his dad for the first time in 16 years. Since then, their relationship has become warmer and now, they spend more time together. For example, they love fishing. Dan’s Instagram is full of Cameron and his sister’s photos while the man always states that he is very proud of his son. 

Gina Dallas (mother)

Date of Birth: December 7, 1968

Zodiac Sign: Sagittarius 

Having German and Mexican roots, Gina worked as a representative of a paper company. She raised two kids single-handedly in Chino, California, after separating from her spouse Dan. Cameron’s success influenced her and the woman also turned into an active social media user. Together with her son and daughter, Gina participated in Dolce & Gabbana’s Spring/Summer 2019 Fashion Show. Being very close with her son, she often gets cool presents from him. For instance, Cameron presented his mom diamond earrings for Christmas.  


Cameron Dallas siblings

Sierra Dallas (sister born to same parents)

Date of Birth: May 12, 1990

Zodiac Sign: Taurus

Having graduated from California State Polytechnic University with a degree in communications, Sierra followed in her brother’s footsteps and launched a YouTube channel, where she shared beauty tips before making vlogs about workouts and healthy lifestyle. She also has an extensive fan base on Instagram and TikTok. Besides, Sierra appeared in Chasing Cameron series and Taylor Swift ft. Kendrick Lamar: Bad Blood Parody. 

Sierra has a close bond with her sibling and that’s not surprising that he walked her down the aisle in June 2018 when she was tying knots with her longtime boyfriend Brent Mallozzi. In 2019, Sierra gave birth to adorable daughter Capri, who is adored by her uncle and is a social media star in her own right despite her young age. 

Cameron Dallas girlfriend

Madisyn Menchaca (girlfriend)

Duration of Relationship: since 2019 till present

Date of Birth: April 29, 2001

Zodiac Sign: Taurus

Madisyn is a model and Instagram star. Her relationship with Cameron started in 2019 and since then, she has been frequently featured in his videos and photos. 

Before, Dallas was romantically linked to many girls, including Rachel Bentley, Hailey Baldwin and Giorgia Caldarulo. 

Cameron Dallas children

Cameron Dallas doesn’t have any kids yet.
