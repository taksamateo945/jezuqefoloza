Louis Tomlinson Confirms Next Single #BackToYou with Bebe Rexha and Digital Farm Animals

 Posted on March 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  376 words  |  Martina Birk

Louis Tomlinson fans, it’s time to rejoice and by that we mean it’s time to pull out all the stops, get your social media accounts ready, and prepare to spread the word because Louis’ next single is coming and it is going to be monumental.

We’ve been waiting a long time to hear new music from Louis. His first solo single was a collaboration with Steve Aoki, Just Hold On, which the pair debuted in December of 2016. The song has staying power and hasn’t lost its impact, but fans were definitely waiting to hear something new. It looks like the wait is almost over if Louis’ latest Instagram post is anything to go by.

The single has been in the works for some time, with Digital Farm Animals posting about Louis on social media months ago and Bebe saying she had something big coming as well, and she just so happened to be in Doncaster at the same time Louis was. Fans put two and two together pretty quickly on that one. However, we are surprised that the song has both Bebe and Digital Farm Animals on the track – but it’s a good kind of surprised.

Bebe is one of the biggest names in music with a unique and powerful sound and Digital Farm Animals are blowing up the scene and making their way to the top. This track will easily be one of the most talked about of 2017.

Louis’ time to shine has been a long time coming and we can’t wait to support him along every step of this new journey. He went from having little to no confidence to realizing that the world is at his feet, and instead of taking advantage of that, he worked hard to prove that he has a voice and a message worth telling. Not that we haven’t known that all along.

We aren’t sure exactly when the tune will drop, but with Louis’ interview coming out Sunday and the post on Instagram, we don’t anticipate we’ll be waiting long to hear the song. We are curious, excited, proud, and feeling extremely lucky to be fans of someone like Louis.

UPDATE: Louis Tomlinson confirmed the single will be released on July 21st in a recent tweet.
